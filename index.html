Boris Brejcha Mumbai gig postponed

By Times of India | March 31, 2023

The Mumbai leg of international artist Boris Brejcha’s India tour stands postponed in wake of rising Covid in Mumbai.

Organizers announced the postponement of the gig scheduled to be held on May 5 at the NSCI DOME, Mumbai. “Due to the current situation, we regret to inform you that the Boris Brejcha show in Mumbai on 5th May 2023 is postponed. The health and safety of our fans, staff and artist are our topmost priority,” the statement read.

Boris Brejcha’s India tour includes shows in Mumbai and Banglore. As of now, there is no word on whether these gigs will go ahead as planned.

We regret to inform you that the event scheduled for 05th May, 2023 has been cancelled due to unforeseen circumstances beyond our control. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.

We would like to bring to your attention that our company has a strict no refund policy in case of event cancellation. We understand that this may be disappointing news, but we hope for your understanding as we have already made extensive arrangements and incurred costs in preparation for the event.

We value your support and hope to have the opportunity to serve you in the future. If you have any further queries or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to us.

Thank you for your understanding.
